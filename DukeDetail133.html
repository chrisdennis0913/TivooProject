Eat Local Challenge

Category: Sustainability

Description: Once a year, Bon Appétit Management Company issues an ¿Eat Local Challenge¿ to all of our chefs. On Tuesday, September 27th, come to the Great Hall, Marketplace or Fuqua's Fox Center and enjoy a lunch that is made completely of ingredients from within a 150 mile radius of Duke. This act, while seemingly simple, has far reaching implications. To read more about the importance of purchasing local food: www.circleofresponsibility.com
