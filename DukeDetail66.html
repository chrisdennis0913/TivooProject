Woodwind Master Class with Windscape

Category: Arts

Description: Created in 1994 by five eminent woodwind soloists, WINDSCAPE has won a unique place for itself as a vibrant, ever evolving group of musical individualists which has delighted audiences throughout the US, Canada, and Asia. As Ensemble in Residence at the Manhattan School of Music, Windscape brings extensive teaching experience to motivational residencies.
