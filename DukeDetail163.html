Finding Your Niche in Business

Category: Conferences

Description: Come find out about the many career opportunities available in business! The overarching message of the program is that whatever one's passion, career goal or personal dream, business can help make it a reality. Students from all majors and years are encouraged to attend and explore possibilities offered by pursuing a career in business. The program will consist of a panel of diverse professionals in the field of business and close with small industry break out sessions. PANELISTS: Meleata Pinto, Worldwide Product Marketing Manager with IBM; Laura Horrigan, Human Resources; Vickie Gibbs, General Manager, New Media with Capitol Broadcasting Company; Lynn Digirolamo, Supply Chain Management Professional, Global Procurement Contracts Shared Services Team with IBM LOCATION: Smith Warehouse Franklin Humanities Center in The Garage, Bay 4 1st floor.
