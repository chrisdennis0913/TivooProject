North Carolina Biotechnology Center Networking Event for Ph.Ds and Postdocs

Category: Other

Description: Join Biotechnology Industry leaders in a variety of fields with fellow PhD students and Postdocs from Duke and UNC at the North Carolina Biotechnology Center Networking event. Build connections with local biotech and pharmaceutical company leaders, learn about their own career paths and how you can best position yourself for a career in industry. Other guests will include scientists, business owners, entrepreneurs, consultants, CEOs, venture capitalists, tech transfer specialists, and patent specialists. This event is a networking and education opportunity, not a career fair. Refreshments will be provided and registration is required. ***Register by clicking More Info at the top. LOCATION: North Carolina Biotechnology Center in Research Triangle Park. Students are responsible for their own transportation.
