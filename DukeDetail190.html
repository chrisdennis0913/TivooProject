DukeWELL Outreach Session

Category: Other

Description: DukeWELL would like to invite all Duke Employees with Duke Select / Duke Basic insurance to come out and visit us at the Duke Hospital Raleigh Cafeteria to find out about your FREE wellness benefits. Members who qualify can earn $150 towards their medication copays after completion of the 90-day care management program. We can also facilitate wellness referrals to other Duke Wellness programs. For more information, contact the DukeWELL office at (919) 419-2660 or by email at pdcdukewellprogram@notes.duke.edu.

Link: http://www.hr.duke.edu/benefits/wellness/dukewell/index.php

Start: 10:00 on September 28, 2011.

Finish: 23:15 on March 4, 2012.
