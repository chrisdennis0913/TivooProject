Durham, NC: Introducing Students to the Community in which We Live, Learn, and Serve

Category: Other

Description: What role did Duke¿s Allen Building play in the Civil Rights History of Durham? What can we learn about Durham from 2010 Census data? In what ways do Duke¿s service-learning courses participate in Durham¿s Downtown Revitalization Projects?
