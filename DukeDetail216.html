Jazz @ the Mary Lou

Category: Arts

Description: Fill your Wednesday evenings with live jazz and the exquisite company of jazz lovers from campus and community in an energetic and engaging atmosphere. Enjoy live performances by local musicians as well as Duke music students, with special guest artists all brought together by John Brown, Director of the Duke Jazz Studies Program. Light hors d¿oeuvres and liquid refreshment will be provided with the option to enjoy late night fare and drinks in the Faculty Commons provided by Plate & Pitchfork. Jazz nights begin at 9:30pm and end at 12:30am. You can stay for just one set or for the whole program, it is an informal environment. Below you will find the dates that Jazz is set for the 2010-2011 Academic Year.
