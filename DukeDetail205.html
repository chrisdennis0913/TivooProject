SOLIMENA Kick-Off Meeting

Category: Other

Description: The Student Organization for Legal Issues in the Middle East and North Africa will be holding a kick-off meeting. The meeting will offer an opportunity for interested students to learn the organization's goals and objectives for the coming year. In addition, students can indicate their interest in serving as 1L representatives and learn the application process. For more information, please contact Daniel Koehler at daniel.koehler@duke.edu.
