School of Medicine New Faculty Orientation

Category: Conferences

Description: SoM New Faculty Orientation is designed to give those who are relatively new to Duke an overview of research, education, and career development topics. The day also includes sessions on the appointments, promotion and tenure process at Duke. Faculty in attendance will have a chance to meet and interact with school leaders during orientation. Participants include the Dean and Vice Deans of the School of Medicine. Please register online.

Link: http://medschool.duke.edu/modules/eguide/event.php?eid=156

Start: 7:45 on September 27, 2011.

Finish: 23:15 on March 4, 2012.

Location: Searle Center Lecture Hall
