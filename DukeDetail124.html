Endocrinology Case Conferences

Category: Local

Description: This event is held in the Barrus Conference Room, Room 3031. The endocrinology case conference gives fellows the opportunity to present one to two clinical cases in-depth, followed by discussion and a presentation of teaching points or relevant literature. The conferences provide an opportunity to discuss important aspects of clinical endocrinology. Correlation with endocrine biochemistry, physiology, and pathophysiology is an integral part of the conference. For more information, contact 919-684-4005.
